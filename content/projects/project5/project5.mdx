# Residential Interior Design Project

## Project Overview

This residential interior design project was developed for a private residence located in Filidoro, Addis Ababa. The objective was to create a comfortable, elegant, and functional living environment that reflects the client's lifestyle while maximizing the use of available space. The design was completed within a one-week project timeline.

## Design Concept

The concept focused on creating a warm and contemporary home environment through the use of clean lines, balanced proportions, natural materials, and carefully selected finishes.

### Design Objectives

- Create a welcoming and comfortable living environment
- Optimize space utilization and circulation
- Enhance natural and artificial lighting
- Integrate functional and aesthetically pleasing furniture layouts

## Key Design Features

### 1. Space Planning & Layout

- **Living Area**: Comfortable seating arrangement for family and guests
- **Dining Area**: Elegant dining space for formal and informal meals
- **Kitchen**: Functional kitchen with efficient workflow
- **Bedrooms**: Private and comfortable sleeping spaces
- **Bathrooms**: Modern and functional bathroom design

### 2. Furniture Layout

- **Living Room**: Sofa, coffee table, TV unit – Comfort and entertainment
- **Dining Room**: Dining table, chairs – Family meals and gatherings
- **Kitchen**: Cabinets, countertops, island – Food preparation and storage
- **Bedrooms**: Bed, wardrobe, nightstands – Rest and storage
- **Study Area**: Desk, shelving – Work and study

### 3. Material Selection

- **Natural Wood**: Flooring, furniture, wall panels – Matte/Textured finish
- **Porcelain Tiles**: High-traffic areas, bathrooms – Anti-slip finish
- **Premium Fabrics**: Upholstery, curtains – Various textures
- **Glass**: Windows, partitions, tabletops – Clear/Frosted finish
- **Natural Stone**: Countertops, accent walls – Honed/Polished finish

### 4. Lighting Design

- **Ambient Lighting**: Overall warm illumination
- **Task Lighting**: Focused lighting for reading and work areas
- **Accent Lighting**: Highlighting artwork and architectural features
- **Natural Light**: Maximized through strategic window placement
- **Decorative Lighting**: Statement fixtures for visual appeal

## Design Services Provided

- **Interior Concept Development**: Initial concept and design direction
- **Space Planning**: Efficient layout and circulation planning
- **Furniture Layout Design**: Furniture placement and selection
- **Material and Finish Selection**: Curated material palette
- **Lighting Design**: Comprehensive lighting plan
- **3D Visualization and Presentation**: High-quality renders for client presentation

## Design Process

### Project Timeline (1 Week)

**Day 1-2: Concept & Planning**
- Client consultation and brief analysis
- Design concept development
- Space planning and layout creation
- Mood board and material selection

**Day 3-4: Design Development**
- Furniture layout finalization
- Color palette and finish selection
- Lighting design plan
- 3D modeling start

**Day 5-6: Visualization**
- Photorealistic 3D rendering
- Material application and texturing
- Lighting simulation
- Interior detailing

**Day 7: Presentation & Finalization**
- Final 3D visualization renders
- Client presentation and feedback
- Design refinement
- Final deliverable package
