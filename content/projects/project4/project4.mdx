# Link Medical Consultancy

## Project Overview

Link Medical Consultancy is a 35 m² healthcare facility located in Bole, Addis Ababa. The project involved the complete interior design and construction of a modern consultancy space tailored to provide a comfortable, professional, and patient-friendly environment. The design focused on maximizing functionality within a compact footprint while maintaining a clean and welcoming atmosphere.

## Design Concept

The concept was inspired by the principles of simplicity, efficiency, and professionalism. The interior was designed to promote patient confidence and comfort through the use of clean lines, bright finishes, efficient lighting, and carefully planned spatial organization. Every element was selected to support both healthcare operations and a positive visitor experience.

### Design Objectives

- Create a professional and welcoming healthcare environment
- Maximize functionality within a limited floor area
- Improve patient comfort and accessibility
- Establish a clean and organized interior layout
- Integrate durable and easy-to-maintain materials

## Key Design Features

### 1. Modern Reception & Waiting Area

- **Reception Desk**: Welcoming front desk with efficient workspace
- **Waiting Area**: Comfortable seating for patients and visitors
- **Patient Flow**: Clear circulation path for easy navigation
- **Display Area**: Space for health information and materials

### 2. Efficient Consultation Room

- **Consultation Desk**: Professional workspace for medical consultations
- **Patient Seating**: Comfortable seating for patient comfort
- **Examination Area**: Private space for basic examinations
- **Storage**: Built-in cabinetry for medical supplies

### 3. Custom-Designed Storage & Cabinetry

- **Built-in Cabinetry**: Maximizes available space
- **Medical Storage**: Organized storage for medical supplies
- **File Storage**: Secure storage for patient records
- **Display Shelving**: Showcase health information materials

### 4. Integrated Lighting System

- **Ambient Lighting**: Overall illumination for comfort
- **Task Lighting**: Focused lighting at consultation desk
- **Accent Lighting**: Highlighting key areas
- **Natural Light**: Maximized through window placement

## Space Planning

### Layout & Zones

- **Reception & Waiting**: Welcoming entrance with seating – 12 m²
- **Consultation Room**: Private consultation space – 15 m²
- **Staff Area**: Workspace for medical staff – 5 m²
- **Storage**: Medical supplies and records – 3 m²
- **Total Area**: 35 m²

### Patient Journey

1. **Arrival**: Warm welcome at reception
2. **Waiting**: Comfortable seating in waiting area
3. **Consultation**: Private consultation with healthcare professional
4. **Check-out**: Efficient check-out and follow-up scheduling
5. **Exit**: Clear path to exit
