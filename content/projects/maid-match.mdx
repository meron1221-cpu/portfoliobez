MaidMatch is an AI-powered platform designed to connect families with professional maids and nannies. The service offers personalized matching based on lifestyle preferences, values, and home dynamics, ensuring a harmonious fit between families and domestic helpers.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Matching**: Utilizes an AI-driven quiz to match families with suitable maids or nannies based on daily routines, support needs, and home vibes.
- **Personalized Profiles**: Provides detailed profiles of domestic helpers, including experience, personality traits, and availability.
- **Direct Communication**: Allows families to connect directly with potential matches, facilitating transparent and pressure-free interactions.
- **Stripe Integration**: Secure one-time payments processed through Stripe, with no automatic renewals, ensuring a straightforward transaction process.
- **Lifestyle-Based Matching**: The platform considers factors like whether a family prefers a calm and quiet environment or a more energetic and flexible atmosphere when making matches.
- **Transparent Pricing**: Clear pricing plans with no hidden fees, offering families control over their spending.

## Technologies

- **Frontend**: Built with Next.js and TypeScript (TSX) for a modern, fast, and strongly typed frontend experience.
- **Backend**: Developed using Laravel, providing a robust and scalable backend infrastructure.
- **AI Matching Algorithm**: Custom AI algorithms designed to assess and match families with suitable domestic helpers based on detailed lifestyle inputs.
- **Payment Gateway**: Integrated with Stripe for secure and efficient payment processing.
- **Styling**: Utilizes Tailwind CSS for a clean and modern design, ensuring a user-friendly experience across devices.

MaidMatch revolutionizes the way families find domestic help by combining AI technology with a user-centric approach.
