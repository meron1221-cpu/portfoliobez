# Yekatit 12 Medical College Cafe

## Project Overview

The Yekatit 12 Medical College Café is a 410 m² hospitality space designed to serve students, academic staff, healthcare professionals, and visitors within the college campus. The project focused on creating a modern, comfortable, and efficient dining environment that encourages social interaction while supporting high daily occupancy.

## Design Concept

The design concept was creating a vibrant and welcoming cafe environment that promotes relaxation and community engagement. Warm materials, contemporary finishes, decorative lighting, and open seating arrangements.

### Design Objectives

- Create an inviting and functional cafe atmosphere
- Establish clear circulation and service zones
- Develop a flexible space suitable for individual users and group gatherings

## Key Design Features

### 1. Contemporary Cafe Interior

- **Modern Hospitality Standards**: Contemporary cafe interior with modern hospitality standards
- **Multiple Seating Configurations**: Seating arrangements for individuals and groups
- **Feature Ceiling**: Decorative ceiling design with visual appeal
- **Warm Finishes**: Warm wood finishes combined with neutral color tones
- **Decorative Lighting**: Statement lighting fixtures as design elements

### 2. Space Planning & Layout

- **Seating Area**: Open seating for students and visitors
- **Service Counter**: Efficient order and pickup station
- **Kitchen Area**: Food preparation and storage
- **Staff Area**: Break room and storage for staff
- **Waiting Area**: Casual seating near entrance

### 3. Ceiling Design

- **Feature Ceilings**: Decorative ceiling treatments in key areas
- **Suspended Elements**: Modern suspended ceiling features
- **Acoustic Treatment**: Sound-absorbing panels for noise control
- **Lighting Integration**: Integrated lighting within ceiling design

### 4. Lighting Design

- **Ambient Lighting**: Warm, welcoming glow throughout the space
- **Task Lighting**: Focused lighting at service counter and tables
- **Accent Lighting**: Highlighting architectural and decorative elements
- **Decorative Lighting**: Statement fixtures that serve as art pieces

## Design Services Provided

- **Interior Concept Development**: Initial concept and design direction
- **Ceiling Design**: Feature ceiling design and detailing
- **Lighting Design**: Comprehensive lighting plan
- **Material and Finish Selection**: Curated material palette
- **Photorealistic 3D Visualization**: High-quality renders for client presentation

## Space Analysis

### Seating Capacity

- **Main Dining Area**: Individual tables and chairs – 60 seats
- **Group Seating**: Communal tables and benches – 30 seats
- **Lounge Area**: Comfortable sofas and armchairs – 20 seats
- **Counter Seating**: Bar-style seating – 10 seats
- **Total Capacity**: 120 seats

### Material Palette

- **Natural Wood**: Flooring, wall panels, furniture – Matte/Textured finish
- **Porcelain Tiles**: High-traffic floor areas – Anti-slip finish
- **Acoustic Panels**: Ceiling treatments – Fabric-wrapped finish
- **Premium Fabrics**: Upholstery – Various textures
- **Glass**: Partitions, display cases – Clear/Frosted finish
- **Metal**: Light fixtures, hardware – Brushed/Powder-coated finish
